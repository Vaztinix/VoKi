Terms of Service (ToS) & Privacy Policy

By using VoKi, you agree to the following terms and policies. These outline how VoKi operates, how data is handled, and the expectations for responsible use.

1. Purpose

  • Provide AI-driven moderation and community tools for Discord servers.
  • Support server safety, privacy, and engagement.

2. Responsible Use

  • Use VoKi features respectfully and within intended scope.
  • Do not exploit, abuse, or harass others using VoKi tools.
  • Follow Discord’s Terms of Service in addition to VoKi’s.

3. AI Features

  • AI assists moderators but does not replace human judgment.
  • AI actions must be reviewed by authorized users.

4. Privacy Policy

  • VoKi does not store personal data unless explicitly configured by server admins.
  • Logs and moderation records are kept private and local to the server.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.
  • Users may request deletion of stored data via server admins.

5. Permissions

  • VoKi operates based on permissions granted by server admins.
  • Unauthorized users cannot access sensitive features.

6. Guidelines

  • Respect other users and VoKi’s systems.
  • Report bugs or misuse promptly.

7. Logging

  • Moderation actions may be logged for accountability.
