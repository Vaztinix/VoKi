Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

By using VoKi, you agree to these terms and conditions. This page clarifies your responsibilities, rights, and how your data is protected.

1. Purpose

  • Provide AI-assisted moderation and community tools for Discord.
  • Support server safety, privacy, and responsible automation.
  • Direct users toward secure and positive community interaction.

2. Responsible Use

  • Users must act respectfully and use VoKi within intended purposes.
  • Do not use VoKi for harassment, spam, or unauthorized data collection.
  • All users must adhere to Discord’s Terms of Service.

3. AI Features

  • AI assists moderation, decision-making, and support, but human oversight is required.
  • Authorized moderators can review or override AI actions.
  • AI recommendations aim to maintain fairness and transparency.

4. Privacy Policy

  • VoKi does not collect personal data without consent.
  • Stored moderation or logging data remains within the server.
  • No data is shared or sold externally.
  • Users may request deletion of stored data via server admins.

5. Permissions

  • VoKi only requests permissions needed for moderation and automation.
  • Server admins maintain full control of bot access and privileges.

6. Guidelines

  • Follow community standards and respect VoKi systems.
  • Report bugs, misuse, or feature requests responsibly.

7. Logging

  • Moderation actions may be logged locally in the server for transparency.
  • Logs help with dispute resolution and moderation accountability.

8. Security

  • VoKi employs secure coding practices and permission handling.
  • Admins are encouraged to review access and command permissions periodically.

9. Updates

  • VoKi may be updated to improve functionality, security, or AI moderation capabilities.
  • Major changes will be communicated via Discord server announcements.

10. Support

Need assistance or have concerns? Visit our support server.
