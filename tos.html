Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

By using VoKi, you agree to these terms and conditions. Please review them carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities while using VoKi and its features.

1. Purpose

  • Provide moderation, AI assistance, and reliable community tools for Discord servers.
  • Support server safety, privacy, and responsible automation.

2. Responsible Use

  • Users must act respectfully and within the bot’s intended scope.
  • VoKi is not to be used for harassment, data mining, or server disruption.
  • All users must follow Discord’s Terms of Service.

3. AI Features

  • AI systems assist moderation and user support but require human oversight.
  • AI decisions can be reviewed and overridden by authorized moderators.

4. Privacy Policy

  • VoKi does not collect personal data without user or admin consent.
  • Data stored for moderation or logging is kept secure and private.
  • No data is shared or sold to third parties.
  • Users may request deletion of stored data through server administrators.

5. Permissions

  • VoKi requires only the necessary permissions for moderation and automation.
  • Server administrators retain full control over bot access.

6. Guidelines

  • Respect other users and VoKi’s systems.
  • Report issues, misuse, or bugs responsibly.

7. Logging

  • Moderation actions may be logged locally within the server for accountability.

8. Security

  • VoKi employs secure practices and permissions handling.
  • We recommend verifying command access and permissions regularly.

9. Updates

  • VoKi may receive updates to improve security, functionality, and AI moderation.

10. Support

Need help or have concerns? Visit our support server for assistance.
