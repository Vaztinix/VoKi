FAQ

How is my data protected?
VoKi only stores necessary data. Admins cannot view messages, and users can delete all data at any time.
How do I request deletion of my data?
Contact us via Discord or email to request complete data removal. Deletion is permanent.
Will features change over time?
Yes! VoKi is continuously updated with new commands and improvements.
