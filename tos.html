Terms of Service (ToS)

Welcome to VoKi. By using our bot, services, and any related tools, you agree to the following terms. These terms explain how VoKi interacts with users, how data is handled, and the expectations for responsible use. Please read carefully.

1. Purpose of VoKi

VoKi is designed to provide AI-driven moderation and community management features. Its core purposes include:

  • Enhancing server safety through moderation tools.
  • Providing AI-powered assistance for community engagement.
  • Ensuring privacy and transparency in data handling.
  • Supporting communities with optional features like logging and Roblox integrations.

2. Responsible Use

Users are expected to act responsibly when using VoKi:

  • Use commands only for their intended purpose.
  • Do not abuse moderation tools or AI features to harass or target others.
  • Respect all community members’ privacy.
  • Follow Discord’s Terms of Service in addition to VoKi’s ToS.

3. AI and Automation Features

VoKi's AI features are designed to assist, not replace, human judgment:

  • AI interactions occur only when triggered by authorized users.
  • AI will analyze flagged content to provide suggestions or summaries.
  • AI results should be reviewed by human moderators before taking action.
  • VoKi does not proactively monitor private channels without explicit activation.

4. Privacy Commitment

We follow a strict privacy-first approach:

  • All data is stored locally within the server unless explicitly configured otherwise.
  • User messages are not sent to external servers unless necessary for optional integrations.
  • Logs of moderation actions, AI responses, or flagged content remain confidential.
  • No personal information is shared or sold to third parties.

5. Permissions and Access

VoKi operates based on permissions assigned by server administrators:

  • Roles and access levels determine who can perform moderation actions.
  • Unauthorized users cannot access sensitive logs or AI controls.
  • Server admins are responsible for granting appropriate permissions and monitoring their use.

6. Community Guidelines

  • Respect other users and VoKi's automated systems.
  • Do not attempt to bypass restrictions or exploit features.
  • Do not use VoKi to collect personal data or violate user privacy.
  • Report any bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior to VoKi developers promptly.

7. Logging and Data Handling

VoKi may log activity based on server configuration:

  • Edits, deletions, and flagged content may be stored for moderation purposes.
  • Logs remain private and accessible only to authorized staff.
  • Data retention periods are determined by server administrators.

8. Roblox & Other Integrations

Optional integrations, like Roblox tools, follow the same privacy-first rules:

  • All data stays within the integration’s scope and server permissions.
  • Users must use integrations responsibly and not violate external platform policies.
