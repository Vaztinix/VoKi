Terms of Service (ToS) & Privacy Policy

Welcome to VoKi. By using our bot, services, and related tools, you agree to the following terms and policies. These outline how VoKi operates, how data is handled, and the expectations for responsible use. Please read carefully.

1. Purpose of VoKi

VoKi is designed to provide AI-driven moderation and community management features. Its core purposes include:

  • Enhancing server safety through moderation tools.
  • Providing AI-powered assistance for community engagement.
  • Ensuring privacy and transparency in data handling.
  • Supporting communities with optional features like logging and integrations.

2. Responsible Use

  • Use commands only for their intended purpose.
  • Do not abuse moderation tools or AI features to harass or target others.
  • Respect all community members’ privacy.
  • Follow Discord’s Terms of Service in addition to VoKi’s ToS.

3. AI Features

  • AI assists moderators but does not replace human judgment.
  • AI analysis occurs only when triggered by authorized users.
  • AI suggestions must be reviewed by human moderators before action.
  • VoKi does not proactively monitor private channels without explicit activation.

4. Privacy Policy

VoKi follows a strict privacy-first approach:

  • All data is stored locally within the server unless explicitly configured otherwise.
  • User messages are not sent to external servers unless required for optional integrations.
  • Logs of moderation actions, AI responses, or flagged content remain confidential.
  • No personal information is shared or sold to third parties.
  • Users may request deletion of stored data by contacting server administrators.

Data Retention: Logs and records are retained only as long as necessary for moderation and may be purged by server administrators at any time.

Children’s Privacy: VoKi is not intended for use by individuals under 13. Server administrators are responsible for compliance with applicable age restrictions.

5. Permissions

  • VoKi operates based on permissions assigned by server administrators.
  • Unauthorized users cannot access sensitive logs or AI controls.
  • Admins are responsible for granting appropriate permissions and monitoring their use.

6. Community Guidelines

  • Respect other users and VoKi’s automated systems.
  • Do not attempt to bypass restrictions or exploit features.
  • Do not use VoKi to collect personal data or violate user privacy.
  • Report bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior promptly.

7. Logging & Data Handling

  • Edits, deletions, and flagged content may be logged for moderation purposes.
  • Logs remain private and accessible only to authorized staff.
  • Data retention periods are determined by server administrators.

8. Integrations

Optional integrations, such as Roblox tools, follow the same privacy-first rules:

  • All data stays within the integration’s scope and server permissions.
  • Users must use integrations responsibly and not violate external platform policies.

9. Security

  • VoKi uses industry-standard security practices to protect data.
  • Administrators should regularly review permissions and logs.
  • Users are encouraged to report any security vulnerabilities.

10. Updates & Changes

  • VoKi may update features and terms at any time.
  • Continued use of VoKi indicates acceptance of updated ToS and Privacy Policy.
  • Major changes will be announced in the official server or dashboard.

11. Support & Contact

For questions regarding VoKi, its usage, or privacy, contact us at: VoKi.io@outlook.com

By using VoKi, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these terms and policies.
