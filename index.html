FAQ

How do I add VoKi to my server?
Click the "Invite VoKi" button at the top of the page and authorize the bot in your Discord server.
How do I use popular commands?
Use commands like /rank, /set welcomer, /ask, or /support in your server. Ensure VoKi has the correct permissions.
Is my data safe?
Yes! VoKi only stores necessary data. Admins cannot view your messages; you can delete your data anytime.
