FAQ

How do I add VoKi to my server?
Use the “Invite VoKi” button above to authorize the bot into your server.
Is my data safe with VoKi?
Yes, VoKi only logs data locally in your server and never shares it externally.
Does VoKi replace human moderators?
No, VoKi assists moderators with AI tools but final judgment is always human‑led.
